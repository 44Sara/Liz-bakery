Where every slice tells a story – Liz Bakery


    I specialize in creating beautiful, customized cakes and desserts for weddings, birthdays, and special events. From classic buttercream piping to intricate fondant work, I take pride in combining creativity with technique to bring my clients' sweet visions to life.

    My journey began in a small bakery where I developed a deep love for the art of pastry. Over the years, I’ve mastered various decorating styles and baking methods, always striving to improve and stay current with trends. I believe that every dessert should be just as stunning as it is delicious, and I dedicate time to making sure every detail is perfect.

    In addition to my technical skills, I value excellent customer service and love working with clients to design one-of-a-kind treats. Whether I’m leading a team in the kitchen or working one-on-one with a bride, I bring professionalism, warmth, and passion to everything I do. Baking is not just my job—it’s my art, and I’m proud to share it with others.
