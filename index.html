About Me

From a young age, I found joy in the kitchen measuring, mixing, and baking alongside family. What began as a childhood fascination quickly became a calling. I was known for my early knack for flavors and my natural eye for detail, and over time, those playful experiments evolved into a lifelong love for the pastry arts.

With over 10 years of professional experience, I specialize in creating beautiful, custom-designed cakes and desserts for weddings, birthdays, and special occasions. From classic buttercream designs to elegant fondant sculptures, I blend creativity with technical precision to bring each client's vision to life.

What truly brings me joy is working closely with people translating their stories and celebrations into edible art. Whether I’m collaborating with a couple on their dream wedding cake or guiding a kitchen team through a busy event, I lead with warmth, professionalism, and passion. Baking isn’t just my career it’s the way I connect, create, and bring happiness to others, one sweet detail at a time.

Elsa Hailu
